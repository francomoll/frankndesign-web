The Studio

Leadership Team

A multidisciplinary team of designers, architects, BIM specialists, and production managers with decades of combined experience in themed entertainment and hospitality.

Francisco J. Núñez
President

Frank brings over 19 years of experience in the architectural, construction, and design technology industries to his work with themed entertainment and hospitality clients.

He is unwavering in his approach to applying and leveraging new technology and digital design tools to the challenges of the theme park business. His expertise of the tools available and his constant drive to find the best solution to any given problem allows him to service his clients in an unparalleled capacity. Frank also possesses an energetic and empathetic business mind — he listens deeply to the needs of clients, and responds with insightful compassion.

Frank created FrankNDesign to provide solutions to complex industry-specific problems. Under his exceptional leadership and foresight, his company utilizes innovative techniques to deliver projects with a combination of speed, precision, and quality that is unmatched in the industry.

Jeremy W. Grunert
Executive Producer

Jeremy is a veteran of the themed entertainment industry. His early experiences working at major theme parks laid the foundation for an illustrious 15+ year career that led him to success at previous positions with Universal Creative and Thinkwell Group.

Jeremy's work with world-renowned IP partners and brands like Virgin Voyages, Warner Bros., and Harry Potter is showcased in experiences throughout North America, Asia, and the Middle East. He is recognized and respected throughout the industry for his ability to produce immersive, innovative, and award-winning experiences.

At FrankNDesign, Jeremy draws upon his wealth of experience and industry insight to lead strategic planning for the company. With his unparalleled passion for themed entertainment projects and his skillful ability to direct that passion towards delivering real-world results, Jeremy is a key component of FrankNDesign's ability to develop groundbreaking theme parks, attractions, museums, and live events for the world's biggest brands.

Carianne DePasqua
Director of Operations

Carianne DePasqua is a seasoned professional renowned for her expertise in project management, business capture, and creative production. With a Master of Fine Arts in Puppetry and a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theater Technical Design, Carianne seamlessly integrates artistic innovation with precise organizational acumen.

As Director of Operations at FrankNDesign, Carianne orchestrates the company's daily operations with finesse, ensuring streamlined workflows and optimal efficiency. Leveraging her extensive background in project management and strategic planning, she spearheads initiatives driving the company's growth and success.

With a proven track record at Animax Designs Inc. in Nashville, TN, Carianne played pivotal roles in business expansion — as Director of Business Capture, she secured over $27 million in projects within two years. Her tenure as Director of Project Management saw her optimizing team performance and implementing streamlined processes for enhanced operational efficiency. Before Animax, she served as Production Manager and Lead Fabricator at Le Chef Costumier in Burbank, CA, leading artisan teams in crafting character costumes and puppets.

Patrick Witthaus
Design Director

Patrick's contributions to the FrankNDesign team stem from his experiences at architecture and design firms across the United States. He has designed solutions for projects at every conceivable scale — from custom recessed light switches for a home in Los Angeles to a master-planned smart-city on an entirely man-made island in the South China Sea.

These unique experiences translate superbly to the themed entertainment industry, where truly immersive projects must be designed at both the micro and macro scales.

As a leader at FrankNDesign, Patrick's digital skill set — in combination with strict attention to detail, a non-stop work ethic, and a relentless pursuit of effective communication — position him at the intersection of innovation, team leadership, and project-wide correspondence. He is driven by both a professional desire to solve complex problems effectively and a personal passion for creating captivating, memorable experiences for everyone.
